Kaleidoscope

Coldplay

July 14, 2017

Kaleidoscope is the thirteenth EP by British rock band Coldplay, released worldwide on 14 July 2017. It serves as a companion piece to Coldplay's seventh studio album, A Head Full of Dreams.

Band Members and Producers

  • Chris Martin - lead vocals, piano, acoustic guitar
  • Jonny Buckland - electric guitar
  • Guy Berryman - bass guitar
  • Will Champion - drums, drum pad, percussion, backing vocals
